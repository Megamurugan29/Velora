## ✅ Implementation Checklist

### Before Upload
- [ ] All filenames follow convention: `PROD-###-{type}.jpg`
- [ ] All files are JPG format (80-85% quality)
- [ ] Main images are 800x1000px
- [ ] Detail images are 600x800px or 400x400px
- [ ] Files are 150KB or less (except main at 300KB max)
- [ ] Traditional Indian ethnic wear (no Western outfits)
- [ ] Professional photography quality
- [ ] Colors match product descriptions
- [ ] Consistent lighting and background
- [ ] Clear embroidery/detail visibility

### After Upload
- [ ] 6 main images in `images/products/main/`
- [ ] Folder structure matches documentation
- [ ] All paths are accessible
- [ ] Test on shop page (images display)
